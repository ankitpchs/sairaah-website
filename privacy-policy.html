Privacy Policy

May Care Foundation Trust — Sairaah Luther Personal Website
Last updated: 27 May 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this website — the personal site of Sairaah Luther, Youth Founder and Student Ambassador at May Care Foundation Trust — handles any information you share when you use it.

We keep things simple: this site is a personal profile website. It does not sell products, run advertising, or create user accounts. The only way we receive personal information from you is if you fill in the contact form.

By using this site you agree to the practices described below. If you have any questions, the contact details are at the bottom of this page.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily give us through the contact form on the home page. That form asks for:

  • Your name — so we know who we are replying to.
  • Your email address — so we can write back to you.
  • A subject and message — the reason you are getting in touch.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts of any kind. We do not collect your IP address, device information, or browsing behaviour. If you simply browse the site without submitting the form, we receive no personal information about you at all.

How We Use Your Information

The information you submit through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Read and understand what you have written to us.
  • Reply to you by email.
  • Follow up if your enquiry relates to a volunteering opportunity or collaboration with May Care Foundation Trust.

We will never use your details to send unsolicited marketing messages, sell them to third parties, or share them with anyone outside of the Trust's trustees who manage this site on Sairaah's behalf.

Data Storage

Form submissions are processed through a third-party form service (see Third-Party Services below). Your message is delivered to our email inbox and we retain it only as long as it is necessary to respond to and resolve your enquiry.

We do not maintain a separate database of contact form submissions. Once your enquiry has been dealt with, we do not store your details in any system beyond the email exchange itself.

This site is hosted on a third-party hosting platform. Standard server logs may be generated by that platform, but we do not access or process those logs for any purpose.

Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. The contact form is intended for adults, parents, guardians, educators, or organisations wishing to connect with May Care Foundation Trust.

If you believe a child under 18 has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us immediately using the details at the bottom of this page and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Services

The contact form on this site is routed through one of the following third-party form services (exact service depends on how the site is hosted):

  • Formspree — if hosted on a standard web server. Form submissions are processed by Formspree, Inc. before being delivered to our inbox. Formspree's own Privacy Policy applies to data in transit through their service. You can read it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Netlify Forms — if hosted on Netlify. Form submissions are stored briefly by Netlify before we download them. Netlify's Privacy Policy applies and can be read at netlify.com/privacy.

No other third-party services — advertising networks, social media widgets, analytics platforms, or tracking tools — are embedded in this site. Social media links in the footer and contact section are plain hyperlinks that take you away from this site; they do not load any external scripts or share your data until you follow them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — ask us what personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete any personal information we hold about you.
  • Objection — ask us to stop using your information for a particular purpose.

Because we hold very little data (essentially only the email thread from your enquiry), responding to these requests is straightforward. To exercise any of the above rights, simply contact us using the details below.

We will respond to any privacy request within 30 days.
